About Me

A developer who also talks to people — and fixes the hardware.

I'm an Information Technology graduate from Cavite State University with a rare mix of skills: I write software and I keep the physical systems it runs on alive. Day to day I provide first-line technical support — troubleshooting computers, printers, and networks — while also building the web tools that make a business run smoother.

My favorite kind of work sits right where those two worlds meet. At a busy printing shop I saw order tracking done by memory and paper, so I built a web-based order monitoring system that replaced the chaos. I like solving real, tangible problems for the people right in front of me — and I build with AI-assisted development (Claude Code), which makes me faster while I still understand every line that ships.
