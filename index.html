About Me

A developer who also talks to people — and fixes the hardware.

I'm an Information Technology graduate from Cavite State University with a rare mix of skills: I write software and I keep the physical systems it runs on alive. Day to day I provide first-line technical support — troubleshooting computers, printers, and networks — while also building the web tools that make a business run smoother.

My favorite kind of work sits right where those two worlds meet.
